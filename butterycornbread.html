How to Make Homemade Buttery Cornbread

INGREDIENTS: 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, ½ cups of stone-ground cornmeal, ¼ cup of sugar, 1 ½ tablespoons of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of kosher salt, ¾ teaspoon baking soda, 1 ½ cups of milk, ½ cup of vegetable oil, 3 large eggs

Step One

Whisk together flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda. Store in a tightly sealed container in a cool, dry place for up to 3 months.

Step Two

When ready to bake, preheat oven to 400 °F. Transfer mixture to a large bowl and whisk in milk, oil, and eggs until evenly combined. Pour batter into a lightly greased baking vessel and bake as directed below. Let cool slightly before serving.

Step Three

Cooking Times: Mini muffin tin (makes 45): 10 to 12 minutes Standard muffin tin (makes 15): 12 to 15 minutes 10-inch cast-iron skillet: 25 to 30 minutes 8-byǊ-inch baking dish: 30 to 35 minutes 8-byǆ-inch loaf pan: 55 to 60 minutes (tent with foil if browning too quickly)
